Library

Here area a few links related to RasMol and other molecular visualization software, and some places to download molecular files.

RasMol

Open RasMol Site
Created and maintained by Herbert Bernstein. Site for the development and the repository of RasMol software, and derivatives such as RasTop, under an open source license for the benefit of the community of both users and developers.

RasMol Home Page
By Eric Martz at the University of Massachusetts, Amherst, MA. This site contains a must of information about RasMol and many other molecular visualization software.

RasMac v2.5-ucb
The UCB enhanced version of RasMol at the University of California, Berkeley, CA.

RasTop en version Francaise
Une version Française de RasTop developpe par Naoum Salame de l’Institut National de Recherche Pédagogique.

Molecular Files

The RCSB Protein Data Bank
The Protein Database: the single international repository for the processing and distribution of 3-D macromolecular structure data. The PDB, formerly run by the Brookhaven National Laboratory, now has its home at the The Research Collaboratory for Structural Bioinformatics (RSCB).

The Online Macromolecular Museum
The Online Macromolecular Museum (OMM) is a site for the display and study of macromolecules. Operated by Daid Marcey et al. at the California Lutheran University.

Molecules R US
A NIH molecule retrieval utility particularly useful to search the PDB database.
