Tracks data is to be used with the tracks program.  See
http://gna.org/projects/tropicalcyclone/.

All tracks data is the product of the US government and is in the public domain.

  North Atlantic and East/Central Pacific best tracks (HURDAT) come from the NHC:
    http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/pastall.shtml

  South, North Indian, and West Pacific best tracks come from the JTWC:
    http://www.usno.navy.mil/NOOC/nmfc-ph/RSS/jtwc/best_tracks/

  The background image comes from NASA:
    http://visibleearth.nasa.gov/view_rec.php?vev1id=11656
